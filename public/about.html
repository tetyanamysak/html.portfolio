About Me

My name is Tanja and I currently work as a supervisor for bakery store. I really enjoy working in a bakery setting and am looking to expand my baking experience and learn more about other pastries. One of my dreams is to have a bakery of my own one day

A few of the things I do at my current job include baking cookies and making frosting. I also train any new hires and make sure everything in the store is stocked and cleaned. I am able to do what is required for opening and closing the store, as some days I open the store and some days I close the store. I make sure that the store looks great every time a customer walks in.

I really enjoy interacting with customers and helping them choose the perfect dessert that I know they will love!
